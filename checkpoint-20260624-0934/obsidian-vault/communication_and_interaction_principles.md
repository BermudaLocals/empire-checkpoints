# Communication and Interaction Principles

- **Directness**: Provide brutal honesty regarding system status. Avoid optimistic, vague reports on project completion percentages.
- **Truth over Sentiment**: Prioritize technical reality, failed modules, and working features over generalized success claims.
- **Pragmatism**: Prefer file-by-file manual builds or script-based surgery over fragile, automated full-stack generation scripts.
- **Security**: Proactively warn against exposing credentials (tokens, DB strings) in logs or plain text files.
